Student Information

Students Not Enrolled in CCSD

Cobb Virtual Academy is excited to offer online courses to students not currently enrolled in the Cobb County School District. We’re glad you’re here and look forward to serving you. Visit the CVA Registration Hub to view the CVA Course Catalog.

Online Registration

After completing registration, you will receive a confirmation email. Your request will then be forwarded to an approver for verification.

Once approved, you will receive a payment email with instructions. Your Welcome to CVA! Important Login and Orientation Information email will be sent after receiving tuition payment.

This welcome message will include CTLS login credentials and next steps for beginning your course.

Tuition

Half-Credit Course

$300

For one half-credit CVA course.

Full-Credit Course

$600

For one full-credit CVA course.

A payment email will be sent to both the parent and student upon approval of the course request. The email will include a personalized payment link. View the CVA Tuition page for more information about tuition and payments.

Final Exams and End-of-Course Tests

All CVA classes include a final exam.

Non-CCSD students in state Milestone EOC-tested courses will take a CVA final exam instead of the state Milestones End-of-Course test.

Final exams must be completed as scheduled. No makeup dates are available.

Before You Begin

Make sure you understand the full CVA course process.

Before starting a CVA course, students and families should carefully review all registration, payment, technology, and final exam information. Understanding these expectations ahead of time can help students avoid delays, stay prepared, and begin the course with confidence.
